So here is my story...

It all started in the month of April 2014. I was in my 4th semester of B. Tech
and staying in Hostel. It was the time of my semester exam that changed
everything. After appearing 3 papers, the exam schedule got postponed to another
20days due to Election. Without leaving a chance I got back to My Hometown. As
there was lot of time to prepare, I was getting bored doing nothing. Thanks to
mother who doesn’t leave a stone unturned to make me lazy and fat. Well,
Facebook was to rescue for my boredom. Facebook. I had no idea what this thing
was until my best friend created a profile for me without asking me. Well,
anyhow I started spending more and more time on Facebook.

So here came the day when I saw a post titled “The Unfriended Friend Part- 3:
The Pain of Waiting”. I though what kind of post is this. Let’s read. It was
like the follows:

“Few Months Before New Year

                Every drop of tear was telling the story of every single moment
I was away from her. Every unsuccessful attempt of forgetting her. Every thought
of resetting my memory to the state before I met her. Every foolishness I had
done for her. Was she worth of that? Or I was getting overwhelmed. If I was
stupid or she was making me thoughtless. If I was into her or she had made me
crazy. She was different when we first met at the Berhampur Station. She was so
charming, simple yet incredibly beautiful in her nature, manner and way of life.
Her surprise entry into my life. Those wonderful two weeks with her.

And so on……”

I was amazed. The writing was intoxicated. Even the rest of the story was so
fascinating that I searched for the first two part of it. Needn’t to tell the
first two parts here but it’s worth mentioning.

Part 1: The Pleasant Meeting, Part 2: Just The Beginning

It’s like any famous writer. But it was heart touching. At least for me it was.
I checked for the writer, went to his profile and he was from my college. I
noticed he had mentioned a blog there. I visited the blog, went through
different stories. It was kind of good time pass. Wasn’t it? 

I didn’t think much about the author of the blog, because my attention was on
the blog. Funny but True. A person is known by his/her work. Right? Days passed.
Sorry, actually 3 days passed. Now it was my daily schedule to read from his
blog.

I used to get friend request, not for my beauty. But because guys needed a
girlfriend. I usually don’t pay much attention to that. But may be fortunately,
on that day I noticed his friend request. Coincidence? May be, Not sure. Seeing
that I was so happy and excited for some unknown reason that I accepted it
immediately. It was Like what you want is what you get. I am still wondering
about that “WHY”. 

I clicked on message option over his profile, but had second thought,

Me 1: “Why should I message him first?”

Me 2: “But I liked his stories.”

Me 1: “So what he doesn’t know that” 

Me 2: “But He sent me friend request.”

Me 1: “And I accepted.”

Me 2: “Don’t you want to chat with him? Don’t you want to know him?”

Me 1: “Yes, That’s point. I don’t know him. I do want to know him. But He should
message first.”

Me 2: “As you wish. Keep waiting.”

And in this way the battle was won and lost. Every 5 minute I was opening
Facebook and checking any new message or post from him. Nothing. Days passed.
Sorry doing the same mistake again. 2 days passed.

One evening Finally I received a message from him. I realized that day, that was
something called happiness and which was unexplainable. He was very casual. That
same old formal information exchange, bit chit chat and it’s over within 5
minutes. That’s it. My first conversation with him. I expected to receive a
message next day from him. But nothing. Absolutely nothing. Maybe I was over
expecting. How could he not send a message? I mean why would he. He doesn’t know
that I want to chat, right. In the meantime, I returned to hostel.

It was the day before my final paper exam, I got a message from him. Finally,
Again. Even though I had one exam to appear, I wanted chat more, but he was much
worried about my exam than me that he said we could chat after the exam is over.
I was very happy at that time so I continued by telling him that I liked his
stories. He was surprised to hear that I had read all his stories. And how one
hour completed, we had no idea. From that day, we became good friends. He wrote,
I read, we discussed.

Not in far future from that day, once he asked me if I was single or in
relationship. I thought, why today. I wasn’t interested to answer to that. I
tried to ignore. But you know guys. He asked again. And I said, “Yes, I have a
boyfriend”.  Then another question was asked. “Will you marry to your
boyfriend?” I mean what kind of crap question was that. I got irritated and
replied, “What kind of question is that? Of course, If I have a boyfriend then I
will marry him only.” He said, “Ok. Good Night.” Nothing else. No message.

I couldn’t understand what went wrong. I mean how could I say no I wouldn’t
marry him. That’s absurd. And why wouldn’t I marry him, I mean he was my
boyfriend.

Next day, as usual as soon as I woke up I checked Facebook expecting a message
from him. He wasn’t online and neither was there any message from him. Chatting
with him had become such an addiction that I kept checking Facebook the whole
day for a single message from him. The very next day also He was offline. And
next day, then next day. I couldn’t resist myself from sending a message to him.
But unfortunately, he had unfriended me. What in earth made him do that. I
really didn’t expect that. I got very angry and very upset. We were so good
friends. What happened was out of my understanding scope. 

Then came the convincing. Really, why should I bother? Why should I get upset?
Who is he to me? It should not matter. But inside I was missing everything we
had, how small/less that may be. It became my habit to go all over again through
the old chats and console myself.

A month passed. One day I was sleeping/resting due to fever and headache.
Received a message from him in my phone.
