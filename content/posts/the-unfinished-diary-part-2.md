“Well I think u did pretty good job but why did u wait so long for it”

“I love this nature and every creation in it. I get involved in them and love to represent them in some pen and paper, make them alive again in my hand. “Looks like your vision is clear about your ambition and aim” I was enjoying being with her and every passing second I was falling in love with her personality.
