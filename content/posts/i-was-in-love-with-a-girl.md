It was not like the other days of my Life. I waited for this day for 3 years.
Nevertheless of what happened in these days I just remembered what She worth to
me. Well, I had never talked with her, didn’t know where she belonged to. Still
I was crazy about her.

I am not going to tell you about me or about her. I am just making myself
comfortable for what I am going to Do Rather it would be better to say I will do
it at any cost.

Tick tick tick. The second hand of our College Auditorium was breaking the
Silence Now and Then. Everyone’s eye was on the Giant Wall Clock. The Silence
created a Fearful Excitement Environment. Now the College Representative Broke
the Silence by Saying the First Word,” Hello Engineers, Let the Party Begin………..
”

The Balloons were Blasted like There was a war between Russia and America.
Students started Dancing to the rhythm of the music. We five friends joined the
Mesh. Ok, now you might be a little bit confused about –“We Five”. Yes, we were
five Friends. Not Best Friends but also not Just Friends. We were only Friends.
You may define what this Friend word means to you but I had never understood it
and mayn’t be. We were ADARS. Each letter is denoted by the first letter of the
First name. Amay, Debasish, Anish, Ramesh, Saurav.

Swamps of students were there Buzzing, gossiping, fun making, joking, dancing……
As we were not out of that thing so we were talking standing at a corner. I had
my eye on her. She was looking Sweet, cute, beautiful, and gorgeous!!!!!! Unlike
the first day I saw her. She was in a white-orange color Sari. A sweet smile on
her face for what I was dying ever.

Years-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*

It was my third day at the tution. It was raining the whole day. Roads were full
of mud, dirt and of course drain water flowing everywhere. I was enjoying going
to tuition those days. So I started my journey through this city dirt to my
ultimate destination CAREER PLUS coaching Centre. I entered into the building
quickly.

On the room outside Verandah a girl in white top and black jeans was sitting. I
just looked at her and thought she must be the first girl to come tution so
early. And I entered another Room (Lab) where some of my friends were talking
and drying their body, shirt and pants before the only fan there running. That’s
it. I had no intension to know about her and she didn’t seem me attractive.

It’s the first day after a one and half month semester vacation. This time I was
well prepared myself for a good semester treatment. Not to absent in class – My
first Rule. Attentive at classtime-2nd. Try to figure out Lessons. Make friend
and be friendly. Well, I was supposed to do all of them but I was failed in some
of them.

The bus came. I was about to enter into it. I noticed someone was looking at me.
I just glanced and what did I get.  A known face in white dress looking like a
Goddess or an Angel. I couldn’t resist myself from keep looking at her. She must
had noticed that, so she changed her sight. The first time I got attracted
towards her. May be I liked a girl in Simple dress rather than jeans and top or
May be really I started liking her.

From that day, my heart wasn’t in my control. It was like I was living in a
jungle without anyone who felt to be mine. I waited for the next day when to
have her one look. But still I was incomplete. It’s like you are in a jungle and
thirsty. You have water but you can’t drink because it’s poisonous. I was dying
inside. But in the presence of friends I used to get to normal. But at home –
Mom cooks, Brother Studies. I was all alone. Couldn’t say to anyone. And I
rarely shared my secrets with others.

After some days, I made up my mind not to get into this I mightn’t be just into
it. So I realized my heart to hate her. That day was one of my worst day ever. I
forgot to take the lab record which was a great mistake for me at that time.
Then I got again confused. So I just decided to remain calm. And proceed. From
that day I became a silent Lover of Her. She knew me. But I knew she actually
didn’t know me. Why would someone bother about someone among so many students?

You could find her always with JP. That means NP (Nirlipta Panda) and
Jinita Priyadrsini were always together and belonged to same branch. When I
looked at her thrice our eyes met. You know what happens then. If you are
looking someone with her friend by her side then both of them can’t get whom you
are looking at. It causes problem.

In some beautiful day she was sitting on one seat ahead of me. Her chuni was
waving outside the bus window. I just called her, “Excuse me, Will you please
keep your chuni inside the bus?” That’s it, it was our first conversation. Not
our, only ME.

Many days passed like this. That day I found out the profile of Jinita
Priyadrsini .I thought NP must be in her friend list. So I went on searching for
her profile. All in vein. She doesn’t have a Facebook account. I relaxed myself
telling, “Facebook is for them who are not beautiful girls. She is so beautiful.
She doesn’t need a Facebook account.” [The above statement is completely not
true. It just a saying for satisfaction after a lot of trial you get failure.] I
thought of sending a friend request to JP but something stopped me from doing
that. Why would I send her a friend request? If not accepted then I feel guilty.
So I stopped. And So On……..

--*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*Farewell
Day-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*-*

Today I am going to propose her. That’s why I am tense, excited and nervous. If
she didn’t accept? She mightn’t be liking me. If she already had a boyfriend.
And many more questions.

Suddenly I got distracted by a known sweet voice. Oh My God, She is in front of
me. I was already nervous. She made me a heart attack. I felt like I am in
Heaven. I just kept looking in her eye. She started, “Hi Ramesh, this is for
you…. ” Guess what!!!! It’s a letter. Ohh…. A pink letter. I dreamed of it must
be her love letter. But I didn’t expected this from her.

I couldn’t handle myself. Suddenly I heard My Own voice – “I LOVE YOU, Nirlipta”

Happiness in Her face disappeared. She was neither happy nor angry. She just
left that place. I couldn’t understand it. As she didn’t say “NO” So I thought
she might expecting me to propose her in some other way or at other place.

I opened the letter. It says – “Hi Ramesh, I LOVE YOU.” I got stunned there. I
was crying happily. “You look me the way I love it. You say many things without
a word spoken .Every time our eye meets I get shy. But I wish you look me that
way always. Boy proposes Girl that’s Normal. I wish you mayn’t misunderstand me
for proposing you.  Still you can reject me. I will get it, May be I’m not
supposed for you” – Your Angel Jinita

I WAS JUST STANDING THERE LIKE A WALL. I DIDN’T FEEL ANYTHING.TEARS WERE COMING
OUT OF MY EYE AND SPEEDING UP THEMSELVES TO TOUCH THE GROUND AND HUG.JUST MY
HEART WAS BROKEN.AND SOMEONE’S MUST BE.
