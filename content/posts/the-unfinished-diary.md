17th June 2013

I am not sure of what I do. There are people even if don’t have any specific
schedule still work better. It feels lonely. No one is to talk with. If there is
no mystery in you then people lose interest on you. You should be someone people
want to know about still they want more of you. Sometimes I want to keep
secrets, hide everything from the world, sometimes I feel like telling
everything to someone. My behavior, style, talk, conversation with others is
unpredictable especially with girls. I feel secure when alone. I feel lonely in
crowd. Yes, books are my life. I can spend my life reading them. There are some
moments when nothings seems desirable. What is this life? Where am I? Why am I
here? What have I achieved till now? Am I worth to what I have got? Whenever I
have choose between things, I choose the one that is unappropriated one. And
later I have to adjust with what I had decided for.

Self-respect, the one I lack in me. It’s something for which all these fights,
war, broken relationships and also something that makes people successful. It
shows you what you are, what you deserve, and what potential you have within
you. People can go any far for this. I am selfish, rude, ignorant, depressed,
unhappy, alone, mischievous, cheater, liar. I am not what I used to be before. I
am confused about my needs. I plan for my better future but soon I do something
that definitely ruins everything I had planned for. The energy, enthusiasm all
vanishes in a moment and I feel I am not myself anymore, someone else is
controlling me.

Rock Star’s Choice had once told me “Never change yourself. Because if you are
special then only for what you are. Everyone has his/her own personality,
attitude, and behavior. You are different and that’s why people recognize you as
you. You can’t be Mahatma Gandhi or Nehru or Subhas or Lincoln but yes, you can
do the good thing they did. You can create new life style by modifying the one
you are continuing with. No more than one people can be same A to Z. this is the
nature and this is the truth. You get me, ok”. And I just stood there without
understanding what he was trying to make me understand.

Happiness is precious. It must be celebrated. Sorrow can never stop following
you. So just let the sorrow pass away and happiness to come. You never know how
any of them will invade into your life, needn’t to bother about too. It’s not so
easy to be happy. It’s a long term practice and patience. I never knew how to
show happiness to others. I remain idle when happy and stiff in sorrow. I don’t
feel much difference between them. Many can’t see what is going inside my mind
or heart by just looking at me until I show them what I want to show. I don’t
laugh whole heartedly. Just a smile and that’s also ignorable. The tough task is
to know someone’s feeling for you.

I love mom and kind of support her whenever there is a fight between mom and
dad. Yet I am not a mama’s boy. Sometimes I also disobey her, get angry on her,
and ignore her. Later it hurts me, why did I do that to her? Most of the times
what she says goes right but who cares. She can foresee but I can understand the
importance of her words after everything is finished. Then I have no other
choice except of repenting.

Dad is always strange. I will say I know only 20% about him. He is good for
everyone but too good for some of the people that they get jealous to him and go
against. Don’t know the history but now the home won’t be silent any more if you
