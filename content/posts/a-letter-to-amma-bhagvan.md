Dear Amma Bhagavan,

This is the worst situation I have ever encountered in my journey of this life.
Though there is always confusion about your existence but still my family
believed in you. As you can see that our current situation is in a volatile
state. For someone’s fault we are getting into trouble. You always say to have
faith on you but what do we get, may be sometimes I get angry on you but what
does my parent did to you, they always favoured in you for their whole life. How
can you so still when your devotees get into trouble. From my birth to today I
can’t see them fully hearted happy. Always compromise, adjustment, don’t they
have the chance to get a good life. A beautiful house, a garden, own furniture,
two bedroom, one kitchen, a dining hall, a drawing room, separate rooms for each
of their sons. What do we want ? Just a peaceful life but what do we get ?
Trouble, Problems and Hurdles. We never wanted someone get problem because of us
and neither we get problem from others. But where ever we go people goes against
us. They can never tolerate our good going family life. We wanted to save some
money so that it can be used when required. But you also got jealous of us. She
ran away with our 1.5 lakhs of money. And now the only expectation is from the
second chitfund. May be we can get 1lakh from it so that 6000 for mausa, 6000
for Santosh bhai, 10000 for katinga headmaster, 15000 plot registration, 38000
kanha’s college fees, 60000 for bike, 10000 new rented home advance sums up to
1.45lakhs. Again dad has to pay the loan instalments 10000 from his 15000
salary. Home rent and current bill comes around 4200 each month. What do you
think god , don’t you have idea about this?
