I am really confused about myself, my decisions, my life, and my wishes. Yes, it
was something between us. May be from my side only. But still it was there in my
heart. I can’t forget the first eye meeting, the look, her cute face, slim sweet
adorable body structure. Her pink color smooth lips. Her pony tail hair. Her
body fragrance. Her cute innocent smile. Whenever I came across her, my heart
used to skip a bit. I might have died several times. I thought I can’t live
without her. How was I going to talk with her? How could I make her mine? Would
have she accepted me? There were many questions revolving inside my mind. Heart
said to explore whatever feelings I had for her that moment. Mind told not to be
stupid. Yes of course heart is stupid. It was a daily fight between mind and
heart. Every day I promised myself not ever to think about her and also not to
look at her. But soon I felt like breaking it passionately. I couldn’t have that
much of strength to avoid my heart’s belief on my love to her. There wasn’t even
a second when I didn’t think about her. She was perfect, more perfect for me but
I wasn’t. I hate myself when I think of being loved by her. It was a pure
foolproof dream and I was enjoying being with her in my dream. Yes we were more
than just friend in my dreams. We were a couple. We shared our thoughts, ideas,
life, happiness, sorrow together. We fought and didn’t talk. The very next
moment we promised never to fight again. But a new fight. It just continued for
infinity. If I could have that power then I wished to be in my dream only rather
than being so far in reality. It gave me pleasure. Those moments were special. I
forgot what to eat, why to eat, reading, friends, home…everything was
unnecessary. I used to stay lying on her lap for hours. She used to tell her
past, her family, her study, her future, her expectations, her wishes, her
belief. I used to listen her like a small kid. “Wake up FOOL, it’s just a dream”
Someone always used to destroy my dreams. I am not saying that she will be Miss
India if participate in the contest but she is perfect for me, at least I think
so. There are many beautiful, smart, hot girls outside. But when she is nearby I
don’t feel anyone’s presence. I didn’t feel the same feelings that I had for
her.

TIME PASSED. Now I ask myself... “Do I really love her? Or it’s just lust.
Attraction to her body. Attraction of a boy to a girl?” I was dying for her.
Still I do now. But Do I love her? I used to listen from people that someone
deep in love forgets to eat, forgets to drink, forgets the world, smiles always,
blush, lost in his/her world. I don’t feel like any of them. I do my job as
usual. I am what I used to be. May be a slight changes can be noticed but not
satisfactory to prove that I’m in love. So I am not in love with her anymore.
REALLY? Then why do I think of her when someone asks about my love, girlfriend,
life, future? Why do I skip a bit when I see her? Why does my heart still
believe in her? Why does it say that she will be mine? MINE ONLY.
