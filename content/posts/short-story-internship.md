Characters:

 1. Avik: The Writer
 2. Tasha: The Intern

Scene:

> Avik and Tasha are sitting in Avik's Office. Avik is typing away the last
> paragraph of the 6th chapter of his book and Tasha is erasing the whiteboard
> that was having the ideas of the 6th chapter.

Conversation:

Tasha asked, "I am free on Feb 14th. Do you want me to accompany you to write
the next chapter of your story?"

Avik replied, "I thought you had a boyfriend. Don't you?"

Tasha was taken aback by his answer. She kept wondering if he was joking around
or trying to be ignorant about her feelings toward him.

Tasha hesitated for a moment before responding, "No, I'm actually single. I just
thought maybe we could work on the book together. I really enjoy helping you
with it."

Avik realized his mistake and quickly apologized, "I'm sorry, Tasha. I didn't
realize you were single. Of course, I would love for you to help me with the
next chapter on Valentine's Day. It would be a great opportunity for us to focus
on the story and not be distracted by anything else."

Tasha smiled, feeling relieved that he had not rejected her outright. She was
still hopeful that he might one day feel the same way about her as she did about
him.

They both returned to their work, but Tasha couldn't help but feel a little
giddy at the thought of spending Valentine's Day with Avik. She couldn't wait to
see what the future held for them.

As Tasha erased the whiteboard, she couldn't help but steal a few glances at
Avik. She had a crush on him for a while now and couldn't help but feel a little
nervous around him. Tasha was single and had been waiting for the right moment
to tell Avik how she felt. With Valentine's Day coming up, Tasha was hopeful
that this could be the perfect opportunity for them to take their relationship
to the next level. She couldn't wait to see what the future held for them.

As Avik typed away, he couldn't help but notice Tasha's glances in his
direction. He had always thought of her as a great colleague and friend, but he
never realized that she had feelings for him. He couldn't help but wonder if she
would be interested in going on a date with him on Valentine's Day. He decided
to take a break from his work and turned to Tasha with a smile. "Hey Tasha,
how's it going? Do you have any plans for Valentine's Day?" He asked. Tasha's
face lit up and she felt her heart beating faster. She couldn't believe that
Avik was asking her out.

Tasha quickly replied, "No, I don't have any plans yet. Why do you ask?" Avik
hesitated for a moment before mustering up the courage to say, "I was wondering
if you would like to go out on a date with me on Valentine's Day?" Tasha's heart
skipped a beat, she had been waiting for this moment for so long. She smiled and
said, "I would love to, Avik." Avik was relieved and felt a weight lifted off
his shoulders. They both returned to their work with renewed energy, excited
about what the future held for them.
