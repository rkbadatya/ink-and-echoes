The relationship between her and my parents is not satisfactory. Mom and her
don’t talk with each other. Mom has said some things to her family members,
which she has taken to her heart. She is not going to let it slide under the

know mom. She is not going to change. I know her. She is not going to change.

going to be there always. And, we are living away. Why not just talk a few words
with a smile on your face just for the sake of it? One thing that may or may not
be good about me is I forget if someone has done something bad to me. Those
memories get overridden with time. But mostly female gender is not so forgetful.
They remember events with minute details and take action based on that.
