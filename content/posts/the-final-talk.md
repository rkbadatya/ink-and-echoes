28th December 2014

Morning 8:15 am

“Hi, Good Morning.” She had sent me a good morning wish. As usual, I opened my
Facebook account and there was her message. Instantly I replied with a good
morning to you too message. And waited if she was there but she wasn’t. So I
just left it open and went to attend to my mom helping her cook. I am not a good
cook but I learned a little from my mom as she always told us to be
self-dependent. Even if I wasn’t aware of its benefit then but I can realize how
much mom was right then. I am grateful to her for that. But I never showed my
affection or love to her. She knew I loved her, as she is my mom, and she knows
me better than I do. At this point I am not going to tell more about that but
the most important part of that day. I had activated the mobile notification
feature so after half an hour I got a notification message in my mobile. I was
grinding the Masala mix but I didn’t take much time as I was too much curious to
know if she had sent the message. But to my unluckily, it wasn’t her, but some
other notification which doesn’t worth writing here, so I skip that. It was an
uninterrupted thought of her and what she was doing. And with every succeeding
minute, I could hear myself breathing heavily. Although we had been chatting for
hours since that day, I mean for around three days but still, I had those same
feelings that were dragging me towards her every moment.

“Ronak, Are you out of your mind? How much are you going to grind that? Do you
want to make juice of it?” Mom shouted advising.

“No, mom. It’s just done.” I said and switched off it and went to my room
leaving mom alone to do the rest. That day was my birthday, so I had a lot of
things on my mind but she was all over my mind jamming the plans I had to do. It
was funny that I was planning for my birthday. But I had to because it was my
last birthday which I could celebrate with my friends before graduation. And
also it was special because I was inviting my sister Supriya to it. My friends
were not apart from me so they were helping me with what to do or what not to
do. I was so happy and full of joy. And also my two brothers were with me, in
fact, our whole family was complete again after one year. Occasions like that
are worth celebrating grandly.

It was around 11:23 am when she finally messaged, “How are you? And how come you
woke up today so early?” She knew I was a late riser in the morning as the
late-night movie-watching habit hadn’t been given up.

“There is nothing, just I couldn’t sleep much. And actually, today is my
birthday.” I replied.

“Really?? Happy birthday. But your profile says 28th June to be your birthday.”

“Yes, I know. But in my home, we celebrate it in December as bada bhai comes
home in this month and with the whole family together we celebrate it.”

“Are you having a grand party? Why I am not invited? :@” she complained.

“Not much. But I am just inviting my close friends to my home for lunch. And who
said that you are not invited? You are the special friend I always wanted to
invite.”

“JK, Ronak. How can I come? I am not there. ”

“Does your presence in my heart not enough that you have to come here? If you
want to taste my mom’s cooked food and experience the delicious then you can
book the airplane ticket, I won’t say no to that. :P ”

“Do I really need to come when I am in your heart? I know if you taste it then I
also can feel it. :P”

“Ohhh, really!!! Then you are definitely not welcome here.”

“Ha ha ha, so how is the preparation going on?”

“It’s almost complete. Just waiting for friends and bada bhai also has gone
outside. After they come back, we will start.”

“So can I listen to your celebration over the phone?”

“Of course, sure. If you have that much talk time on your mobile, why will I
oppose you?”

“OK, Give me a miss call when you start the celebration.  I have to go now.”

“Ok, I will,” I said and she went offline. I heard my friends had arrived.
Shreyas and Deepankar with Tushar were just outside my home. I invited them in.
They all were so happy and started Arranging things as soon as they arrived.
After around 10 minutes Ayan and Amresh came and after them came Akshay. Well,
all they were my best buddies. My sister had called that she was on the bus with
one of her friends so it would take her some 10 minutes to come. Everyone in the
home was so in a joyous mood. Shreyash and my younger brother went outside to
bring some of the remaining stuff and the rest of us enjoyed it inside with the
beats of the music. Di had called and I gave her the direction to my home and
waited for her outside my home near the gate. What an angel she was in my life
to come and change me so much. She had that angelic smile and there was Rupeeka
with her. Both of them were dazzling due to the sun rays. I welcomed them inside
before which Di hugged me and Rupeeka handshaked.

Now everyone was there but not Badabhai. The girls were talking among themselves
and boys among themselves. As Badabhai was taking too long to come and also
wasn’t picking up our calls so we started a game which is called, Dumb se Razz.
It was favourite game of Shreyash but I could never succeed in that game in my
whole life. I was just looking at the clock and waiting for brother and also was
praying God to send badabhai soon. And here came badabhai, So it was the time to
celebrate. I miss called her, and she called instantly. I received but didn’t
say a word neither did she. The celebration started after lighting the candles
but mom said not to put off the candles. I cut the cake and offered god after
which we all shared the remaining. I saw my mobile and looked if she was there
on the otherside or not. So I said,”hello”. And she replied, “It was wonderful.”
I was surprised that she was really hearing all our celebrations.

After the cake cutting now it was time for some fun out of the box so we danced
and did every kind of crazy stuff. And soon we got tired. Even though I was
among my friends still my heart was there on the other side of the phone. No one
knew about it, no one noticed. Around 2:30 pm when we had lunch and in that
session, bada bhai asked everyone to tell something about me, good or bad
anything. It was so much fun and also appreciated how they interpreted me in
their view. To them I was surely not a bad guy but also not some famous guy, I
was just their friend and I wanted to hear that only. Now it was time for a gift
discloser. After all, I was the birthday boy and deserved gifts. I opened the
gifts one after another but I got the special one, a guitar which bada bhai had
gifted me, that’s the reason he was so late. I had a broader smile while
unwrapping the gifts. Everyone loves it and I was no exception.

Finally, the celebration finished and friends started going. And when there were
only our family members I was too tired to talk or discuss what happened that
day so I just went to my bed with my phone in my hand. I said, “Hey, are you
there?”

“Yes. You are so lucky to have your friends and such a cute family.”

“yes, I am proud of them. How was it?”

“I liked it. But the gift unwrapping was interesting.”

“Why?”

“Because I like gifts too.”

“What do you like in a gift?”

“Anything, but Once Rahul has gifted me a white color top and black color jeans
which I like much.”

“Ohh, so your favorite color is white and black. Han?”

“No. I like pink and purple. But she had gifted me that with love so it was
special.”

“By the way, who is Rahul?” I asked occasionally.

“He is my Boyfriend. You know his every gift is special for me.”

When I heard that, I was shocked and paralyzed. What did she just say? She had a
boyfriend. Did I hear the same thing that she said? Or it was just some
misconception? I repeated her words several times and realized she said exactly
the same as what I heard. I didn’t say a word but just stayed silent over the
phone. She continued many things about her birthdays and about her boyfriend,
how it was so special for her every time. But I was in no sense to listen to
her. The only thing I was hearing, again and again, was that she had a
boyfriend. How could she have a boyfriend? I couldn’t take it easily, because I
was damn so much in love with her. And now on my birthday, I got to know that
she wasn’t mine anymore. Was that fair? No, it wasn’t. She couldn’t commit to
someone else except me. I saw her first and fell in love with her. She was mine.
But was she really?

I never had asked her about it before. Was that my fault? Was that my fault to
love her so intensely? How could GOD be so unfair to me? I was just started to
enjoy life but I went into hell again. My life was over.

“Hey, Ronak. Are you listening?”

“you shouldn’t have said about your boyfriend.” I was so hurt. I could barely
talk. So I hung up the phone and slept covering my face with the pillow. Drops
of tears were flowing and landing on the bed to wet it slightly more at a time.
My phone rang, but I didn’t pick up. It ranged for the tenth time but I was
indifferent. Then it beeped one message. I didn’t see it. Then another. Now I
went into my mobile inbox and saw it was her message.

“Ronak, what happened? Are you ok? Why aren’t you picking up my phone?”

I had no intention to reply. There was no meaning in replying. It was the
indigestible truth that she loved someone else. I was nothing to her. But with
the increasing messages in my inbox, I couldn’t resist but replied.

“I love you but you love someone else. I can’t be friends with you anymore. It’s
better for both of us to stay away from each other. Goodbye”

“What are you talking about? I like you as a good friend, in fact, you are my
special friend. Why are you so upset about it? It’s true that I have a boyfriend
but it’s not my fault. I love him and so he does.”

“Goodbye,” I was crying. I wanted her to persuade me to stay with her.

“Please Ronak, you are my good friend don’t do this to me. We are close friends
why do you want to stay away from me? Don’t stay away.”

I didn’t reply.

“Don’t hurt me like this. You are so much important to me. You are a part of my
life. Don’t go away now. Please reply. Please.”

No, I couldn’t reply.

“I like everything about you. But I love him so much. I can’t change anything. I
want you in my life. Please reply.”

I switched off the mobile and started sobbing while sleeping.
