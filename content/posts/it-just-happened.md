Another day without you. You mayn’t know my feelings. You mayn’t understand my
intension. I tried my best in regards to have you in me. Myself I dream the
Dream that mayn’t come true. Your heart is beating for me only – I can’t say
this is true. Still my love for you flows in my blood – it is just an
expression.

My parents brought me up to this level. How can i be such a selfish? I had never
thought of them when you were in my eyes. Your love ( Ohh sorry, I don’t know if
you love me or not . May be I am fooling myself considering my one sided love to
as your love to me ) had made me Blind. I couldn’t see them when they were
suffering. I believed in you. Not in my Gods. They were my God when you were not
my angel.

New things attracted me when the old portable, consistent were still in my
support. I misunderstood them and tried to understand you, the unsolvable
problem for whom I can never prepare an Algorithm.

Our first meeting you can forget but I live in these memories. The only input I
have for my soul is your sweet memories after you left me in the End ( Not
Middle ) of our journey. I didn’t dared to keep you near me. May be misfortune
blessed me with sins; you were always there to curse the misfortune and save me
from my only mistake of loving you. You are my first…..No Second….. No Third
Love of my Livelihood. The other two are also in my mind. But you are Primary,
all others are auxiliary actually they are the Backups needed when you get
corrupted or power failure will occur. You knew it. Still loved me.

I love you because you love me much. Still you say I am not selfish!!!!!

I love you because I need you. Still you think I am honest!!!!!

What in this earth made you love so much that after your streaming out I caches
your memories in my L2 cache. I need a hard disk to keep our fight, anger, cry,
shout in it. Fear if it is crashed. No alternatives. You are killing me and so I
am too.

It was certainly my thought to take advancement in the path of sacredness.
