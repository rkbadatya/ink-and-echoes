You have imagined a lot of things and are happy because you think that you are
going to do something that will make your as well as their life beautiful and
full of enjoyment. You get so much involved in your inner joy that you are bound
to do some mistakes and there is the turning point of your life. You get angry
on someone close to you because they do something that is different from usual
or may be that seems sort of uneasy. But you forget that there must be something
that is behind their abnormal behavior. You get angry on someone without knowing
their current circumstances but you do so because you care for them and they are
really close to us. You feel guilty when someone gets hurt for something that
you had done. And it’s heartening when you know it but can do nothing but stand
looking at them while they pretend like being happy and try to cover their inner
cry in them. You just can’t see them unhappy but also can do nothing to make
them happy as you are the reason for it or may be your deeds. You will never
want to be the reason of someone’s problem in life and the only thing you can do
there is ask for forgiveness. Because, your much involvement in that may worsen
the situation. You must back off from there. The most unbearable guilt in the
world is to be the cause of someone’s life crisis knowingly or unknowingly. As
most of us believe that time heals everything, so you should wait for it. And
the solution will be in front of you someday, I swear. It’s not always possible
to stand up to everyone’s expectation and to handle it in front of a mass of
friends is the toughest part. When you are mystery to everyone, you are
interesting but when you’re an open book, people lose interest in you and time
comes when you fell neglected but that’s the truth. Because everyone has their
own life and you can’t/shouldn’t interfere in it. When they think that they are
important in your life but we just ignore them, they feel neglected and leave
you in midway. Why can’t they understand that what we are for them may be or
mayn’t be they for us? Still we are guilt of it because we want them most in our
life. And yeah, that believe that they will come is enough to live the rest of
our life.

It was always the wrong decision I had made in my life. It changed the life of
many beings. I was not prepared or I am not now, who can tell me that? I never
knew. I wanted it and did, but was that to their expectation? It seemed not, as
even I wasn’t satisfied with what I did. I had the mind of a child not a mature
one. I was curious and happy but was nervous and afraid of what next is there
for me, for every one of us. Everyone had their plan but me. Were they happy or
pretending to be happy? I can’t say. When you have nothing to share then what
can you? I thought that it will be easy and smooth but I lacked the vision to
foresee the other side of it. They were there but it felt they were all alone in
side themselves. On passing hours I was losing my patience, and that last moment
was something I had to do something up to my level best. But what could have I
done?
