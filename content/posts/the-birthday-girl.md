“Siku...get up. You will be late for tuition, Beta. Get freshen up and take the
breakfast quick” mom was shouting at me sweetly.

“Yes….I will. Why are you worried about? It only 8:10. And tuition is at 9.” I
was still sleepy.

I had told mom to wake me up as I had this special tuition class today. And the
Alarm rang. So there was no point to sleep more. I got up and brushed. I went
outside to enjoy the view. It was a fine morning. It was 18th August 2013. I had
twenty minutes before going to tuition and had nothing to do. So picked up my
phone and opened Facebook wall to have a quick look at. And I got in my
notification area that it’s Tulika Pattnaik’s Birthday. I went into her profile
and send a message

“Happy bd to bd girl … wish u a wonderful good morning…” 8:27am

Someone had ever told me if really want to wish then don’t post in her wall,
send a message and I follow his path.

Within no time I got reply “thanku” 8:27am 

I was surprised. It was like she was waiting for me to wish her. How come she is
browsing facebook except of doing things that to be done on birth day? So I
wanted to know what actually this girl was.

“So fast to reply!!!!!!!!”

“Hmm” she replied. I had a feeling that this not her bd but the day before her
death sentence.”

“How many wishes u get till now...  500?” I tried to make her honored. I had
never got even more than 10 wishes on my birthday and was asking about 500!!!!!

“Huh!!  Naah i hvnt counted”

So what the hell are you doing in Facebook so early morning? I thought but never
said.

“So…reply to this question carefully ….ok …..Its ur bd that’s why u r
happy...or u r happy as its ur bd?”

“I’m happy bcoz itz my b'day”

I asked the same question twice you fool. Why are you choosing?

“So wats todays plan? ....went to temple in the mng?”

“hvnt yet taken shower”

Whom are you waiting for? Are you a princess or what that the servants will
serve you a bath?

“Did u at least do pranam to papa and mummy and other elder members?”

“naah” 

You idiot. Don’t you have manner also?

“goooooood. Giiiirllllll” I acted like I was her boyfriend which I wasn’t.

“…….” Silence from her side. I was wondering about how she will take it as. So
not letting her think much I sent msg.

“jub cake katogi to fb me post jarur karna.....hum log use dekh kar hi pet
bhar lenge” I tried to be funny.

“Sure “ She was replying straight forward. Nothing more than the answer
required, also in short words only. I didn’t like it. Are you a robot or what?

“Ok bye…going to tuition...” It was almost 8.45 and I also wanted to end this
cold conversation between us which neither have any meaning nor a base.

“Bye” she replied.

I closed my Opera and started toward the tuition by bicycle hanging my bag from
my back.
