29th October 2013 evening

“Are u coming or not?” Santosh almost ordered.

I was not in a mood to talk with anyone or go outside for two days. I preferred
to stay in home only. As the semester was ahead so I was really worried about my
performance this time. The PHAILIN had almost destroyed my hope for good in
exams. I was not prepared well neither my friends. Everyone was getting serious
this time but I couldn’t find myself any serious. I was depressed and hating
myself for what I was. I was not successful. I was not popular. I had no best
friend. I had no one to share what I felt. I was really alone as always. So it
was best for me to give sometimes to me to recover and soon start my
pre-exam-preparations. Santosh used to call me in the morning around 10am but I
didn’t received any of his call rather I put my phone in plane mode. No contact
with outside world. It’s better. So after those two days in the evening he
called me and I had to attend the call. He didn’t listen to my any of the
excuses. He was not angry on me but a little bit show off.

“I don’t feel like coming today” I said like I was dying.

“Don’t you dare say those words again?” He warned. ”Do u forget that we have to
study ACA? Come home soon need to talk with you.”

“Ok. Then after 6.30pm” I replied.

“Can’t u come now? And did they give power supply to your area?”

“Yes power supply only in night.”

“Ohhh… that’s why u r not coming.”

“No not like that yar…just mood off. I will come after 6.30 only. You know
why…..ok”

“Ok see you” he said and call disconnected.
