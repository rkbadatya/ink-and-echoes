Today someone told me that my friends (girls) like me because of my talent not
of my personality. I am now in a dilemma if that was true or was just his/her
own view. And then it came into my mind to analyze how both of those terms are
related to each other and which one is best suited to define me. Yes, the word
talented has dominance over my personality when associated with me. But that
doesn’t mean that I don’t have a pleasant personality. It may not be attractive
or prominent but still, I have it and it’s completely mine. So if someone gets
to know me from my talent then he/she must be missing me in myself. Because I
was always after a decent personality and a simple life with family and friends,
and talent was just for inner peace or enjoyment.
