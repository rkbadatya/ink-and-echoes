When they wanted it, I denied. And when I wanted it, they ignored it or maybe
they were just over with it. They were always there for me and I appreciate
that. But there are some moments when you have to stand alone and I had to. But
this humanly nature also had its part of share where it just pushed me back and
the fear emerged. I have/had this confusing state of mind where I needed support
and suggestion of someone whom I consider as my well-wisher. Though I wanted
something still I needed someone’s encouragement. And I got it too. Till the
last moment I was unsure if I would do that or not, but I did. This was the
first and the last time I was doing it. I fought with myself for the PRECIOUS.
First of all I had a set of ideas to do, which I thought of to be silly so
discarded later. Then some new thought got their place in my mind and it was
possible to be done. But the environment was not what I had expected to be, and
here again I failed myself. I waited, tried for the PRECIOUS. But I had my
schedule which I had to follow and which was much more important than the silly
things I was about to do. I had imagined a 100%, but did 50% and got 10%. That’s
it. I was done with my deeds. I got impatient and returned back from the check
point which was just before the destination. Well, well. I can never be reformed
because, for this unchanged personality I have overcome those barriers which I
suspected to destroy me.
