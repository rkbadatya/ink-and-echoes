Relationship…….

It’s a word I come upon to hear often now a days. People give lots of speech on
it. Gives suggestions. It’s like a western wind that blows everyone who come
upon in its way. Lovers, parents, adults say about it much. I am kind of bored
of this word listening in movies, gossips, and friends’ discussion. Wherever
whenever I think of this word I really get confused.

First I wasn’t aware of it that this word means anything to me. I thought I am
in my own house with parents, brothers. To my unaware I was there because they
were with me. Father for clothes, books, other stuffs like taking me to outside
on walk. Mother for feeding me, letting me to sleep by her side. Brother , a
friend ( actually a good friends who is trustworthy ) for playing game, fighting
like like I was actually where I should be. These are the people whom I deserve
to live with. It was a wonderful feeling. Fearing to Dad , Scolding of Mom ,
Blackmailing of BadaBhai etc. these were not just so.

I didn’t know it was a family although I don’t know what is a family. But what I
knew that they were my own and now they are my very own. It’s probably a
difficult task when you are to be chosen between your Family and your Lover (
Not Love. It’s typical to call your Lover as Love. Then what about your Father,
Your Mother. Aren’t they Love you , Don’t you love them.) that most of the
Lovers face in today’s Life. But the very question remains before me is How can
be your Lover is out of Your Family. And How would you make a family without the
Loved ones.

It’s normal to have a close friend in Life with whom you can share your most
important problems. And for this most of us won’t choose our parents. Amn’t I
right ? That’s the reason why we look for someone else. Well that could be
someone between your school or college friends. It is simple for them who are
open minded and friendly in nature. But for a shy one it’s much more difficult
to have such a friend than toppering the math paper. They look forward for
someone who is different than other and also like own. That’s where we get
trapped. I don’t know how friendship turns into Love. How it starts or how it
ends. I am just looking forward something that concerns to someone of us who
didn’t get the real meaning of it.

Helping , caring , supporting etc. are these the sign of Love. Yes of course.
But can anyone get it in the rudeness of a good heart, fight for your good,
scold for the better ? it’s nothing but a process of life. When you get love you
love others until you don’t.

How big the problem may be Parents stand behind you every time. When father get
angry on you, Mother supports And Father saves you when Mother stars to scold.
Brother let you play his favorite game even though you are banned for failing in
the exam although the other time he fought for breaking down his most
interesting Game DVD. That’s the power of Relationship.  No one can never break
this until his/her own soul is get destroyed by the curse of the deeds.
